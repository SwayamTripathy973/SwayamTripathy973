About Awesome Cat Games

Tired of boring old board games that require no skill and only chance? Want something interesting with a cool story? Awesome Cat Games are for you! From fantasy games to action adventure board games, there's a board game for older audiences of many different genres.

Our Story

Our company promise is to produce high-quality board games that feel complete and provide great value.

How Our Games Work

  • We provide the board as part of the board game, with additional boards being sold separately to change things up.
  • We provide the board pieces with unique stats and custom pieces that you can easily paint.
  • Our board games also come with rule sheets and character lore.
  • We have books for our story that expand on the world of the board games.
  • Our board games will be continuously updated to reflect the changing times.
